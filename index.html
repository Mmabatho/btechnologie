Our Story

Btechnology was founded by a passionate South African Black woman with a vision to close the digital divide and bring reliable, accessible connectivity to homes and businesses across the country.
With a degree in Information Technology and a decade of hands-on experience in the ISP and connectivity space, our founder turned a dream into a thriving business rooted in excellence, innovation, and community empowerment.

What started as a one-woman initiative has grown into a trusted provider of connectivity solutions, serving clients with dedication, technical expertise, and a genuine desire to make technology work for everyone.
